Minato Namikaze

Namikaze Minato was the Fourth Hokage of Konohagakure. He was renowned all over the world as Konoha's Yellow Flash (Yellow Flash of the Leaf).

Itachi Uchiha

Uchiha Itachi was a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan who served as an Anbu Captain. He later became an international criminal, murdering his entire clan. He afterwards joined the Akatsuki.

Madara Uchiha

Uchiha Madara was the legendary leader of the Uchiha clan.

Hashirama Senju

Senju Hashirama was a member of the famed Senju clan. He himself was hailed as the "God of Shinobi" for his unmatched ninja prowess.

Kakashi Hatake

Hatake Kakashi is a shinobi of Konohagakure's Hatake clan. Famed as Kakashi of the Sharingan. After the Fourth Shinobi World War, Kakashi becomes Konoha's Sixth Hokage.
